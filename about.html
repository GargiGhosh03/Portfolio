About Me

I’m Gargi Ghosh, an undergraduate student specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I’m deeply passionate about developing AI-powered applications, designing conversational chatbots, and conducting research in Large Language Models, Deep Learning, and Data Science.

Born in New Delhi and raised in the vibrant city of Durgapur, West Bengal, I’ve been shaped by culture and diversity. Through school and university, I’ve explored both technical excellence and creative pursuits.

From painting and classical dance to karate and chess, I’ve actively represented my school and university, including at the AIU Nationals in Fine Arts.

I’m also a passionate photographer and cinematographer, capturing moments through visual narratives. My love for storytelling led me to publish two novels on Amazon.
